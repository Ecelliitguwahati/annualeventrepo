About the Competition

Internship is an integral part of a student’s learning experience. Many college students don’t get a chance to do an internship because of lack of opportunities. Also many talented students don’t get a job for the same reason. With this vision in mind, the Internship and Job Fair (IJF) is an excellent educational and career-focused event which aims at connecting startup enthusiast graduates/undergraduates to the startups so that they get the relevant experience in their desired field.Don't miss this opportunity to meet & network with representatives from a wide variety of organizations seeking interns and job candidates for immediate and future openings.
