About

The workshops will encompass a whole range of techniques and activities aimed to foster the entrepreneurial ideas among students. Students would be exposed to resources catering to their individual needs. Startup India would be hosting a range of activities to help the students understand the different avenues for growth and opportunities that are being provided. Entrepreneurs would be called upon to share their start-up experience and demonstrate how various tools can help a budding entrepreneur. The whole event would also include an open house where students and entrepreneurs can network, discuss and even ideate.
