Intern Fair


Information for Students

Internships are a fantastic way to gain some hands-on work experience. Having good grades is of utmost importance, but landing on an internship is probably more important to get the most out of it. Learning soft skills such as communication, will greatly expand your network of influential people. Speaking of networks, having a group of influential people around will help you greatly in your future. It’s no longer just about the grades; it's also about your reputation as a hard worker, your ability to sweet-talk an interviewer, and your desire to improve yourself. At this fair you can explore the opportunities open to you and take your first step on the career ladder.


Intern fair planning tips

  1. Consider why you want an internship.
  2. Construct an effective resume
  3. Prepare for on-the-spot interviews. Visit InterviewStream to get interview questions listed by major and job industry, and practice your answers.
  4. Identify your answers to commonly asked questions.
  5. Be sure to customize your resume for each interview opportunity. Nothing screams “lazy” like a generic resume and cover letter!
  6. A good rule of thumb is that it’s always better to overdress than underdress, so don’t worry if you seem more professional than the other candidates.
  7. Prepare your elevator speech beforehand, which should detail who you are, what your goals are and why you are a great candidate for the internship. Having an elevator speech ready before your interview can make the difference between an awkward moment and a defining moment within your career.

Information for Employers

If your organization is looking for access to first-year undergraduates, sophomores and penultimate year students from IIT Guwahati seeking spring weeks internships then this fair is a great opportunity for early engagement and to raise your profile with our highly-motivated students. Udgam wants to connect IIT Guwahati's bright and talented students with employers who are seeking to fill vacancies for jobs or internships in their establishment. This is a wonderful opportunity for you to meet students from across all academic disciplines and class years, and establish a positive presence on our campus.


General Setup


The event is held in the hallways of the Conference Hall. Each employer will be provided one draped table and chairs for up to two recruiters. If you have more than two recruiters, please let us know or register for an additional table. Stationaries will be provided. Bring along the posters of your company for display in the stall.
