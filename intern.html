Intern Fair


Information for Students

Internships are a fantastic way to gain some hands-on work experience. At this fair you can explore the opportunities open to you and take your first step on the career ladder!

REGISTER HERE

Intern fair planning tips

  1. Consider why you want an internship.
  2. Construct an effective resume
  3. Prepare for on-the-spot interviews. Visit InterviewStream to get interview questions listed by major and job industry, and practice your answers.
  4. Identify your answers to commonly asked questions.
  5. Develop a 30 second summary of your professional skills, experience and interests

Information for Employers

If your organisation is looking for access to first-year undergraduate, sophomores and penultimate year students seeking spring week or internship placements during their studies, this fair is a great opportunity for early engagement and to raise your profile with our highly-motivated students. Udgam wants to connect IIT Guwahati bright and talented students, both seniors and underclassmen, with employers who are seeking to fill vacancies for jobs or internships in private industry, science, government, non-profits and education. This is a wonderful opportunity for you to meet students from across all academic disciplines and class years, and establish a positive presence on our campus.

General Setup

The event is held in the hallways of the Conference Hall. Each employer will be provided one draped table and chairs for up to two recruiters. If you have more than two recruiters, please let us know or register for an additional table.
