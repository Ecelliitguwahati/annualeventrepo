Social entrepreneurship hackathon with PRIZES worth Rs 25,000

Hack for Health


About

Udgam IIT Guwahati is proud to host its flagship hackathon for developers and software enthusiasts to participate in the biggest hackathon in North east to create a social impact in the field of healthcare. This is the perfect platform for the technical community to showcase their coding skills to impact real life problems. With exciting prizes and networking opportunities this Udgam iced with entrepreneurial summit will be a lifetime experience.

Hack for health invites you to submit application with a software solution for problems in healthcare industry for round 1 and the selected entries will compete in a 24 hr hackathon executing software preposition and finally present it before the Judges and win prize money worth 25,000.

Event Structure

Round 1 : Idea Submission
  • Teams to submit a proposal letter.
  • One team can submit ONLY One Idea.
  • Submission dates should be strictly followed. No exceptions will be made.
  • Entries to be sent only in the prescribed format; otherwise they are bound to get rejected.
  • Ideas are to be submitted here - hackforhealth@udgam-iitg.in
Round 2 : Hackathon

  • If the team is selected for the final hackathon have to travel to IIT Guwahati for 19-20 January.
  • 24hr hackathon starts on 19th January and ends on 20th January which teams have to present their hacks.
  • A presentation time of 5 min will be allotted for each team followed by 3 min of Q/A session with judges.
  • Extending the time will result in negative points.
  • Participants should get their own laptop.

Rules and Regulations

  • A maximum of 4 participants per team are allowed.
  • Members from different branches of the same college/ institute can form a team.
  • Clearly communicate the e-mails and mobile numbers of all team members as well.
  • College/University Photo ID is mandatory for participating in the finale.
  • The teams which send solutions after the given deadline will be disqualified.
  • All material that is related with the competition will be provided in English.
  • Only the team leader will be contacted for all official purposes. He/She will be responsible for communicating for other team members.
  • UDGAM IIT Guwahati reserves the right to disqualify any Submission it deems inappropriate and remove the submission from the campaign site.
  • The decisions made by UDGAM and/or its appointed judges are final and binding in all manners and no appeals shall be entertained for any reasons whatsoever.
  • A participant should not be older than 25.
  • A participant is NOT allowed to have more than one team.

Important Dates

  3. Start of 1st Round –15th December,2018.
  4. Last day of submission of first round – 27th December,2018.
  5. Results of 1st Round – 5th January,2019.
  6. Final Hackathon – during UDGAM 19.
